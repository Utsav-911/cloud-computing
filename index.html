What is Cloud Computing?

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—like servers, storage, databases, networking, software—over the internet (the cloud). It offers faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.

Key Benefits

  • Cost Savings
  • Scalability
  • Accessibility from Anywhere
  • Automatic Updates
  • Disaster Recovery
